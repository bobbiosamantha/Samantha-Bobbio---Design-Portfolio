About


Hello! My name is Samantha Bobbio and I am a recent marketing graduate of LIM college. I am currently working for a digital media agency as a Search Engine Marketer / Analyst .

Throughout my experience analyzing data and writing ad copy for various clients I realized that my creative aspirations weren’t being answered. Although I enjoyed marketing for the consumer research and testing aspects I found that UI/UX was the perfect way to combine both my creative and analytical skills.

When I’m not designing new wireframes, I love to work on my fine art projects where I usually water color or illustrate with ink pens. I also love TV and am usually binging two shows at once!
